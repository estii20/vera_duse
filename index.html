About Your Practitioner

Meet Vera Duse,
RN · NT · IFMCP

With over 12 years of clinical nursing experience spanning ICU, primary care, and integrative health settings, Vera Duse brings a rare combination of rigorous medical training and holistic perspective to every client she supports.

After witnessing the limits of symptom-only medicine, she pursued advanced training in functional nutrition, yoga therapy, and integrative medicine — determined to offer a more complete model of care that addresses root cause, not just surface presentation.
