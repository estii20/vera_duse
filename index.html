Meet Vera

Helping You Feel Your Best,
Naturally.

I'm passionate about helping people improve their wellbeing through nutrition, yoga and sustainable lifestyle changes. With a background in nursing and a holistic approach to health, I believe lasting wellbeing comes from understanding the whole person rather than simply treating symptoms.

Whether you're looking for personalised nutritional support, restorative yoga, a wellness retreat or healthy catering for your next event, my goal is to create a welcoming space where you feel supported every step of the way.
